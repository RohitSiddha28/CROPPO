Application Guidelines

  • Apply 200-250 kg per hectare
  • Best applied before sowing or planting
  • Split application recommended for long-duration crops
  • Ensure proper mixing with soil for best results

Alternative Recommendations

Urea

High nitrogen content, suitable if soil test shows nitrogen deficiency.

DAP (18-46-0)

High phosphorous content, good for early growth stages.

Organic Compost

Natural alternative that improves soil structure and nutrient content.

Fertilizer Application Tips

Timing

Apply fertilizers at the right growth stage of your crop. Most crops need more nutrients during their vegetative and flowering stages.

Moisture

Apply fertilizers when soil is moist but not waterlogged. This helps in better nutrient absorption and reduces runoff.

Balanced Nutrition

Ensure balanced nutrition by considering secondary nutrients (Ca, Mg, S) and micronutrients along with NPK.

Organic Matter

Incorporate organic matter into soil to improve its structure, water-holding capacity, and nutrient retention.
