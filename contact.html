Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get started with AgriTech services?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account on our website, and you'll have immediate access to our basic services. For premium features, you can choose a subscription plan that fits your needs.

Do you offer on-site consultations?

Yes, we offer on-site consultations for farms within a 100-mile radius of our office. For farms outside this area, we provide virtual consultations via video call.

How accurate is your weather prediction service?

Our weather prediction service has an accuracy rate of approximately 85-90% for 3-day forecasts and 75-80% for 7-day forecasts. We use multiple data sources and advanced algorithms to provide the most reliable predictions possible.

Can I integrate AgriTech with my existing farm management software?

Yes, we offer API integration with most popular farm management software. Our technical team can assist you with the integration process to ensure seamless data flow between systems.
