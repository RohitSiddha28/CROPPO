Tomato Late Blight

Confidence: 95%

Description

Late blight is a destructive disease of tomato and potato that can kill plants within days if weather conditions are favorable and the disease is not controlled.

Recommended Treatment

  • Remove and destroy infected plant parts
  • Apply copper-based fungicides
  • Improve air circulation around plants
  • Avoid overhead watering

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of plants can be analyzed?

Our system can currently identify diseases in over 30 common crops including tomatoes, potatoes, corn, wheat, rice, and various fruits.

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our AI model has an average accuracy of 90% for the most common plant diseases. The accuracy depends on image quality and disease visibility.

What makes a good plant disease image?

For best results, take close-up photos in natural light, focusing on the affected areas. Include both healthy and diseased parts for comparison.
